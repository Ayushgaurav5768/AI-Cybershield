Phishing is a social engineering cyber attack where attackers impersonate legitimate organizations to steal sensitive data such as usernames, passwords, credit card numbers, or authentication tokens.

Common phishing indicators:
- Suspicious URLs with extra characters
- Misspelled domain names
- Urgent language like "Act Now"
- Fake login pages
- Requests for confidential information

Types of phishing:
- Email phishing
- Spear phishing
- Whaling
- Smishing (SMS phishing)
- Vishing (Voice phishing)

Phishing attacks often use fake HTTPS certificates to appear secure.
Users should verify domain authenticity before entering credentials.
